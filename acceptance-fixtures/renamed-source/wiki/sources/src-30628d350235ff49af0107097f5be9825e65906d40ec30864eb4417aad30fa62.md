# Research Note: Held Entry Policy

Finance reviewers asked for held entries to stay visible without affecting posted balances. The
recommended baseline is to exclude held entries from balances and count them as exceptions.

## Claim

Held entries should not change reported balances until they are posted.

## Evidence

The sample data includes disputed and manual-review entries that would make the daily cash and
receivables balances misleading if they were included.
